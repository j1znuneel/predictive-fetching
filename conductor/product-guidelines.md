# Product Guidelines: PrefetchAI

## Design Vision
PrefetchAI aims for a "invisible" intelligence that enhances performance without demanding attention. The design philosophy centers on functional clarity and high-confidence automation.

## Tone and Voice
- **Welcoming & Efficient**: Documentation and user-facing messages should be approachable and helpful. We prioritize clarity and developer productivity, ensuring that the complex math behind predictions is explained in a friendly, digestible manner.

## Visual Aesthetic (UI/UX)
- **Minimalist & Clean**: Any UI components, debug visualizers, or demo applications should feature clean lines, generous whitespace, and functional clarity. Avoid visual clutter to ensure that the focus remains on the "magic" of predictive loading.

## Core UX Principles
- **High-Confidence Accuracy**: The primary mandate for the predictive engine is to minimize false positives. We would rather miss a prefetch opportunity than waste network resources on a "blind" guess. Every trigger must be backed by strong kinematic or behavioral evidence.
- **Predictive Harmony**: The system should feel like a natural extension of the user's intent, anticipating needs without creating a sense of being tracked or monitored.

## Developer Experience (DX)
- **Zero-Config Start**: The library must provide sensible defaults that work immediately for the majority of use cases.
- **Type-Safe & Scalable**: Full TypeScript support is mandatory, ensuring that integrations are robust and scalable across large codebases.
- **Transparent Logic**: Developers should have access to clear logging and optional visualization tools to understand why a specific prediction was made, facilitating trust and easy debugging.
